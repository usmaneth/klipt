08 — Do / don't
Do
✓Use the mark on dark or off-white only
✓Always lowercase: klipt
✓Clear space = 1× inner square on all sides
✓Red for decisive moments only — the cut, the CTA
✓Use Inter or system sans-serif
Don't
✕Never use the mark on a colored background
✕Never title-case: Klipt
✕Never stretch, rotate, or recolor the mark
✕Never add shadows or outlines to the mark
✕Never use red for anything except the cut accent
