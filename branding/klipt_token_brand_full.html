06 — Do / don't
Do
Use the mark on dark or off-white only
Always lowercase: klipt
Let the outer rings breathe — clear space = at least one ring width on all sides
Red for decisive moments only — the cut, the CTA, the status
Don't
Never put the mark on a colored background
Never title-case: Klipt
Don't use red for anything other than the cut accent
Never add outlines or drop shadows to the mark
